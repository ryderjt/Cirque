Collective

We archive moments, chase light, and craft essays that linger in the quiet.

Cirque is a collective dedicated to storytelling through film — blending original cinematography with archival imagery to reflect on time, memory, and meaning. Our work is guided by curiosity for overlooked histories, tactile processes, and contemplative pacing.

Ryder Thomas

Director of photography. Architect of light, crafting contemplative frames that breathe like still photographs.

Rafa Cortes

Editor and researcher. Weaves archival fragments and contemporary footage into cinematic essays.

Sebastian Neisic

Sound designer and colorist. Shapes atmosphere with layered field recordings and analog processing.
